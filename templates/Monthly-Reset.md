# Monthly Reset Protocol

> **Goal:** High-level system calibration. Remove bottlenecks, upgrade processes, and align engineering output with long-term career goals.

## 🟥 PHASE 1: Audit & Remove (De-cluttering)
*Identify what is draining energy or providing zero value.*

- [ ] **Technical Debt Audit:** What "temporary" solutions are now causing friction?
- [ ] **Commitment Review:** Which recurring meetings or low-impact responsibilities can be delegated or cancelled?
- [ ] **Tool Cleanup:** Remove apps, extensions, or subscriptions that didn't help your performance this month.
- [ ] **Stop-List:** Define at least one habit or behavior to stop next month.
    * *Example: "Stop checking Slack during the first 2 hours of work."*

---

## 🟩 PHASE 2: Optimize & Improve (Refinement)
*Upgrading existing protocols based on last month's data.*

- [ ] **Metrics Analysis:** Review your "Predictability Score" and "Deep Work Hours" for the past 4 weeks. What is the trend?
- [ ] **Bottleneck Analysis:** What was the #1 reason for delays this month? How can the system prevent it?
- [ ] **Playbook Update:** Adjust your `Daily-Checklist.md`. Add a new check or remove an irrelevant one.
- [ ] **Skill Sharpening:** Identify one technical area (e.g., "Memory Profiling") to improve based on recent tasks.

---

## 🟦 PHASE 3: Strategy & Add (Expansion)
*Setting the trajectory for the next 30 days.*

- [ ] **The "North Star" Goal:** Define one major engineering achievement for next month.
- [ ] **System Expansion:** Add one new "Module" to your CRAFTER OS (e.g., a new template for ADRs or PR Reviews).
- [ ] **Networking/Reputation:** Who in the industry or company should you connect with next month to build social capital?
- [ ] **Resource Allocation:** What new book, course, or tool will you integrate into your workflow?

---

## 🟨 PHASE 4: Anti-Fragility Check
*Preparing for the unexpected.*

- [ ] **Risk Assessment:** What is the biggest potential threat to your productivity next month? (e.g., big release, vacation, new team member).
- [ ] **Mitigation Plan:** How will the system absorb this "stress"?
