# Weekly Review Protocol

> **Goal:** Synchronize results with intentions and recalibrate the system for the upcoming week.

## 🏆 PHASE 1: Wins & Achievements
*Analyze what went right and why.*

- [ ] **Major Deliverables:** List the key PRs, documents, or decisions completed this week.
- [ ] **Systems Wins:** Where did CRAFTER OS work perfectly? (e.g., "Deep Work allowed me to finish Task X in half the time").
- [ ] **Small Wins:** List minor technical or process improvements that made life easier.
    * *Example: "Optimized my IDE shortcuts for faster navigation."*

---

## ⚠️ PHASE 2: Failures & Signal Noise
*Honest audit of system breakdowns.*

- [ ] **Predictability Gap:** Which tasks were not finished and what was the *root cause*? (Poor clarity, external pings, or estimation error?)
- [ ] **Signal Noise:** List the main distractions that broke your Focus (Meetings, Slack, "Quick questions").
- [ ] **Technical Debt:** Where did you trade quality for speed this week?

---

## ⚙️ PHASE 3: System Improvements
*Immediate updates to your Daily Playbook.*

- [ ] **The "One Fix" Rule:** What one change to your process will prevent last week's biggest failure?
    * *Example: "If requirements are vague, I will schedule a 10-min sync BEFORE starting execution."*
- [ ] **Tooling/Environment:** Does anything in your workspace (digital or physical) need adjustment?
- [ ] **Documentation:** Update your internal docs or `README.md` if a new pattern was discovered.
- [ ] **ADR Synchronization:** - Did we change any architecture/tools this week?
  - Is there an ADR for this? (If not, create one in `docs/adr/` using the template).
- [ ] **The "One Fix" Rule:** What one change to your process...

---

## 💡 PHASE 4: Strategic Ideas & Backlog
*Capturing creative sparks without losing focus.*

- [ ] **Future Projects:** Technical ideas or refactorings that aren't urgent but are valuable.
- [ ] **Knowledge Growth:** What did you learn this week that should be explored further?
- [ ] **System Evolution:** Ideas for new modules or templates for CRAFTER OS.

---

## 📅 PHASE 5: Next Week Planning
*Setting the stage for a high-performance week.*

- [ ] **Top 3 Objectives:** Define the "Must-Achieve" goals for the next 5 days.
- [ ] **Calendar Defense:** Protect your Deep Work slots. Decline or move conflicting meetings.
- [ ] **Reputation Goal:** What one thing will you do to increase trust with your team/stakeholders?

---

### 📝 Weekly Summary (Example)
- **Wins:** Successfully migrated the Auth module. Reputation score is high.
- **Failures:** Missed the Wednesday goal due to a "critical" bug that wasn't critical.
- **Improvement:** Added a "Severity Verification" step to my Daily Checklist.
- **Idea:** Automate the generation of this Weekly Review using a simple script.
- **Next Week:** Focus 100% on the Database optimization task.
