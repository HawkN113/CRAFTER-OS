# 🚀 Onboarding Checklist (The First 48 Hours)

### Day 1: Mindset & Principles
- [ ] Read **[CRAFTER.md](../CRAFTER.md)** to understand the core framework.
- [ ] Review **[Engineering.md](../Engineering.md)** for cultural expectations.
- [ ] Set up "Quiet Hours" (Dark Mode) in Slack/Teams for Deep Work blocks.

### Day 2: Execution & Tools
- [ ] Clone **[Daily-Checklist.md](./Daily-Checklist.md)** to your personal workspace.
- [ ] Execute one 2-hour Deep Work session on a low-complexity task.
- [ ] Submit your first PR following the "Definition of Done" standards.

### End of Week 1: Calibration
- [ ] Perform your first **[Self-Assessment](../Self-Assessment.md)**.
- [ ] Meet with your mentor to review your **Predictability Score**.
- [ ] Read the last 5 **ADRs** to understand current architectural decisions.
