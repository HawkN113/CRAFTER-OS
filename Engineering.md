# 🧠 Engineering Culture (CRAFTER-Based)

Engineering culture is built on the CRAFTER principles.

---

## Core Expectations

An engineer:
- think clearly
- maintains reputation
- adapts to change
- stay focused
- improves technical mastery
- executes reliably
- takes responsibility

---

## Behavior Model

- results > activity
- decisions > discussions
- simplicity > complexity
- adaptation > rigidity

---

## System Thinking

An engineer treats:
- tasks as systems
- errors as signals
- processes as optimization targets

---

## Final

Culture is not declared.
It is repeated behavior.
