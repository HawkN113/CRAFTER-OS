# 👥 Roles & Applicability Matrix

CRAFTER OS is a modular system. While it was forged in the trenches of Software Engineering, its principles of **Clarity, Focus, and Execution** scale across the entire product organization.

---

## 🥇 Primary Roles (Full Integration)
*These roles require 100% of the CRAFTER Protocols to maintain high-complexity output.*

### **Software Engineers / Senior Devs**
* **Focus:** Deep Work, Technical Mastery, and Execution.
* **Core Tools:** [Daily Playbook](../Playbook.md), [Self-Assessment](../Self-Assessment.md).
* **Goal:** Maximize high-quality code output and minimize architectural debt.

### **Tech Leads / Engineering Managers**
* **Focus:** Reputation, Responsibility, and Adaptation.
* **Core Tools:** [Team Roadmap](../Team-Roadmap.md), [Metrics Layer](../Metrics.md).
* **Goal:** Ensure team predictability, protect engineer focus, and scale the culture.

### **Architects**
* **Focus:** Cognitive Clarity and Systems Thinking.
* **Core Tools:** [CRAFTER Principles](../CRAFTER.md), [Monthly Reset](./templates/Monthly-Reset.md).
* **Goal:** Simplify complex requirements into executable technical roadmaps.

---

## 🥈 Secondary Roles (High Synergy)
*These roles benefit from the "Low-Noise" communication and "Definition of Done" protocols.*

### **DevOps**
* **Focus:** Anti-Fragility and Responsibility.
* **Application:** Treating infrastructure as a system that requires the same "Mastery" and "Clarity" as product code.

### **QA**
* **Focus:** Reputation and Execution.
* **Application:** Standardizing the "Definition of Done" to ensure zero-friction handovers between Dev and QA.

---

## 🥉 Partial Applicability (Interface Layer)
*Roles that interact with the engineering "Engine". They must respect the system's boundaries.*

### **Product Managers (PMs)**
* **Focus:** Cognitive Clarity and Adaptation.
* **Requirement:** Respecting "Deep Work" blocks and providing "High-Signal" requirements to avoid re-work.

### **Product Designers**
* **Focus:** Focus and Simplicity.
* **Requirement:** Aligning design "Sprints" with the engineering execution cycle to maintain momentum.

---

## ☁️ Limited Applicability (Support Layer)
*Administrative roles that benefit from the resulting organizational stability.*

### **HR / Recruiting**
* **Application:** Using the [Engineering Culture](../Engineering.md) document as a benchmark for hiring and performance reviews.
* **Goal:** Identifying "CRAFTER-minded" talent who value autonomy and discipline.

## 📜 The Universal Protocol

Regardless of the role, everyone interacting with a CRAFTER-based team must follow the **Noise Reduction Rule**:
> "Communication must be **Asynchronous by default** and **Synchronous only when necessary** to protect the collective Focus of the system."
