## Case 3 — The "Hero Culture" Trap (Anti-Case)

**Context:** A high-performing senior engineer in a fast-growing startup.
**Symptoms (Without CRAFTER):** - Working in "firefighting" mode constantly (Lack of Cognitive Clarity).
- Juggling 10+ open tasks (Lack of Focus).
- Declining trust from stakeholders due to missed deadlines (Loss of Reputation).
  **Outcome:** Burnout within 6 months, high technical debt requiring 40% of the system to be refactored, and eventual resignation.

**Key Takeaway:** CRAFTER is insurance against "unsustainable heroism." It replaces individual sacrifice with systemic reliability.
