# 🤝 Code of Conduct (CRAFTER OS)

The **CRAFTER** community is built on engineering excellence, professional trust, and the pursuit of high-performance outcomes. Our communication is a reflection of our systems: it must be high-signal, low-noise, and results-oriented.

---

## 💎 Core Values in Communication

* **Clarity:** Be precise. Ambiguity is the enemy of progress.
* **Respect:** Value the time and focus of others.
* **Responsibility:** Own your words and their impact on the system.

---

## ✅ Expected Behavior (High-Signal)

We encourage a culture of **Radical Candor** and technical integrity:

* **Constructive Feedback:** Critique the *architecture* or the *logic*, never the person. Provide a path to improvement with every critique.
* **Direct Communication:** Use the shortest path to the solution. Avoid corporate jargon or passive-aggressive tone.
* **Focus on Improvement:** Every discussion should move the system toward a more "Antifragile" state.
* **Data-Driven Arguments:** Support your claims with metrics, logic, or documented experience.

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior (System Noise)

Behavior that creates cognitive friction or degrades the community's performance will not be tolerated:

* **Toxic Communication:** Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
* **Personal Attacks:** Shifting the focus from the technical problem to the individual's character.
* **Unproductive Discussions:** "Bikeshedding" (arguing over trivialities) or repetitive circular debates that provide no execution value.
* **Focus Theft:** Tagging individuals unnecessarily or disrupting deep-work cycles without a critical reason.

---

## 🛡️ Enforcement & Governance

The maintainers of **CRAFTER OS** are responsible for protecting the system's integrity:

1.  **Reporting:** If you encounter behavior that violates these principles, report it to `nselyutin@gmail.com`.
2.  **Warning:** For minor "noise" violations, a direct warning will be issued to recalibrate behavior.
3.  **Removal:** Severe or repeated violations (toxicity, harassment, or persistent disruption) will result in a permanent ban from the project and its resources.

---

## 📜 The CRAFTER Axiom

> **Professionalism is the discipline of keeping the focus on the mission.** > **If it doesn't help the community build better systems, don't say it.**
